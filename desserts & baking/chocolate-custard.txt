CHOCOLATE CUSTARD

\ Ingredients

- Water, 2 cups (or 1 cup water, 1 cup coffee)
- Custard powder, 30g
- Chocolate, 100g

\ Method

In a saucepan on medium heat, whisk together water and custard powder.
Keep whisking until visibly thickened, then remove from heat.
Let cool for a couple of minutes, then melt in and integrate chocolate.
Refrigerate.
