CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

\ Ingredients

- Maple/golden syrup, 2 tbsp
- Neutral oil, 1/2 cup
- Oat milk or water, 1/4 cup
- Sugar, 150g
- Vanilla, arbitrary amount
- Flour, 280g
- Baking powder, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Chocolate chips, 120g

\ Method

Whisk all ingredients except flour and choc together until homogenous.
Mix in flour, taking care not to overmix.
Add choc.
Refrigerate for at least 30 min.
Shape into 15 cookies of approx. 50g each.
Bake at 170c convection for 18 min.
