TOFU TIKKA MASALA

\ Ingredients

- Oil, 1 tbsp
- Ginger, fresh, 1-2 tsp
- Garlic, 4-5 cloves
- Curry powder, 1 tbsp
- Garam masala, 2 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Ginger, ground, 1/2 tsp
- Onion powder, 1/2 tsp
- Paprika, 1/2 tsp
- Cayenne pepper, pinch
- Tomato passata, 700g-ish g
- Water, 300-ish ml
- Chickpeas, rinsed, 1 can
- Tofu, 1 block
- Soy sauce, 1 tbsp
- Balsamic vinegar, 1 tbsp
- Coconut cream, 1 small can
- Salt, to taste

\ Method

Fry ginger and garlic in oil for a couple minutes.
Add all spices and toast for a minute.
Add passata and water, bring to boil, then simmer for 15 min.
Add tofu and chickpeas and cook for another 5 minutes.
Remove from heat, add remaining ingredients, then salt to taste.
