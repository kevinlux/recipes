OAT MILK

\ Ingredients

- Rolled oats, 1 + 1/2 cup
- Sunflower oil, 2 tbsp
- Vanilla paste, 1 smidgen
- Guar gum, 1/4 tsp
- Salt, 1 small pinch
- Water, 3 - 4 cups, depending on desired consistency

\ Method

Blend all ingredients for 10 seconds in a high speed blender.
Line a big jug with a milk bag, then pour the mixture in.
Squeeze out all the liquid, then pour from jug into a bottle.
