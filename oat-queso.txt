OAT QUESO

\ Ingredients

- Water, 2 cups
- Rolled oats, 1 cup
- Nutritional yeast, 1/2 cup
- Cornstarch, 1/2 tsp
- Garlic powder, 1 tsp
- Salt, 1/2 tsp
- Liquid from pickled jalapeno jar, 1/3 cup
- Dijon mustard, 1/2 tsp
- Cumin, 1 tsp
- Gochujang, 1/2 tsp
- Miso paste, 1/2 tsp
- Olive oil, 2 tbsp
- Lime/lemon juice, 2 tsp

\ Method

Blend all ingredients in high speed blender for half a minute.
Then transfer to saucepan and bring to simmer until thickened.
Remove from heat and serve.
