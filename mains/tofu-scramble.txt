TOFU SCRAMBLE

\ Ingredients

- Olive oil, 1/2 tbsp
- Firm tofu, 1 block
- Nutritional yeast, 1-2 tbsp
- Salt, to taste
- Turmeric, 1/4 tsp
- Garlic powder, 1/4 tsp
- Non-dairy milk, 2 tbsp

\ Method

Heat olive oil in pan over medium heat.
Crumble tofu into pan and cook out moisture (3-4 min).
Add turmeric and garlic powder, then cook for another 5 min.
Pour in non-dairy milk, add nutritional yeast and combine.
Remove from heat and serve.
