TVP BOLOGNESE

\ Ingredients

- Olive oil, 1-2 tbsp
- Onion, 1-2
- Garlic, minced, 2-5 cloves
- Italian herbs, 2 tbsp
- Cayenne pepper, small pinch
- Tomato passata, ~700g
- Red wine vinegar, 1 tsp
- Gochujang, 1 tsp
- Marmite, 1 tsp
- TVP, 200g
- Water, 3-4 cups
- Salt, to taste

\ Method

Over medium-high heat, fry onion in oil until translucent.
Add everything else, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for at least 20 min.
