KIDNEY BEAN AMERICAIN

\ Ingredients

- Kidney beans, 1 can
- Garlic powder, 1 tsp
- Parsley, dried, 1 tsp
- Pepper, to taste
- Vegan dijon mustard, 1 tsp
- Mayo, 2 tbsp
- Vegan Worcestershire sauce
- Ketchup, 1 tbsp
- Tabasco, to taste
- Capers, handful
- Pickled cocktail onions, handful
- Salt, to taste

\ Method

Mash beans, then mix in remaining ingredients.
